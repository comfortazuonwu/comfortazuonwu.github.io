2021’s first case of West Nile Virus found in Bear Lake County

EastIdahoNews.com Staff

Southeastern Idaho Public Health officials have confirmed identification of the first-ever West Nile Virus positive mosquito pool in Bear Lake County, located on the south end of the county close to the Utah-Idaho border on the west side of the lake. This is the first WNV-positive mosquito pool in SIPH’s eight counties for 2021.
